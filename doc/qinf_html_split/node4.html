3 Representation of states and operators

Kets are represented by matrices, bras by matrices. The objects are represented in the computational basis. Bras and kets representing the same states are related by Maxima's conjugate transpose function ctranspose Density operators and other operators are represented by matrices. The tensor product is represented by the Kronecker product. There is no strong typing. You are responsible for knowing that a particular vector represents a state vector in a particular space. Below, we often substitute ``the operator'' for ``the matrix representing the operator''.
